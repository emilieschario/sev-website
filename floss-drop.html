Floss Picks Are the Unsung Hero of Dental Care

Most people know they should floss daily, but only 30% actually do it.[2] Traditional floss can feel awkward and inconvenient. Floss picks offer a simple solution that makes flossing easier and more likely to become a habit.[3]

Ease of Use = More Consistent Flossing

Traditional floss is awkward to use and hard to maneuver, especially for back teeth. Floss picks have a handle that makes the job easier and faster. When something is simple to do, you’re more likely to do it every day. Floss picks make flossing as easy as brushing your teeth.

Better for Busy Lifestyles

Traditional floss needs both hands and a mirror, making it hard to use when you're busy or on the go. Floss picks are portable—keep them in your car, desk, or purse. You can floss anywhere, anytime, like after lunch at work. When the tools are handy, you're more likely to use them.

Encourages Kids (and Adults) Who Hate Flossing

Kids struggle with regular floss and often give up. Floss picks are easier for small hands to grip and come in fun colors and flavors. They're also more comfortable for adults with sensitive gums who don't like wrapping floss around their fingers.

More Precise Cleaning

Traditional floss only works well with perfect technique, which most people don't use. Floss picks keep the floss tight and make it easier to angle properly, so you clean between teeth more effectively.[3]

Encourages a Floss-First Mindset

When flossing feels hard, people skip it.[4] When it's quick and easy, it becomes a habit. Floss picks help you go from thinking "I should floss" to just doing it naturally.

Without the environmental impact

Traditional floss picks create plastic waste—a valid concern for eco-conscious users. The Floss Drop solves this problem by using 100% home compostable materials. You get all the convenience of a floss pick without harming the environment. Good oral health shouldn't come at the planet's expense.
